## Introduction of New Medical Programs

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has issued a formal notice regarding the introduction of new programs in the Medical and Para Medical disciplines. These courses are set to commence from the Academic Session 2026-27, providing expanded opportunities for students in the healthcare sector.

## Admission Details

While specific eligibility criteria and seat counts for each new program will be detailed in the upcoming admission brochures, prospective students should prepare for the standard university selection process. For a comprehensive overview of how to apply to the university, you can refer to the [IPU admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php).

## Stay Updated

Candidates are advised to monitor the official university website for further details regarding entrance tests or merit-based admission schedules for these new offerings. If you are also considering technical or professional postgraduate courses, you may check the latest updates on [MCA admission IPU](/mca-admission-ipu.php) for the current cycle.
