## New Healthcare Programmes for 2026-27

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has released a notice regarding the introduction of new medical and paramedical programmes for the upcoming 2026-27 academic session. The official notification was issued on April 30, 2026.

## Information for Prospective Students

Candidates interested in pursuing medical or paramedical studies at IP University should prepare for the upcoming admission cycle. For a detailed overview of the university's admission process, you can consult the [IPU admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php).

Additionally, candidates may find it helpful to review previous years' [IPU CET cutoff](/ipu-cet-cutoff-2025.php) data to understand admission trends for similar medical programs. Specific details regarding the curriculum and seat intake for these new courses will be provided in the official admission brochures at ipu.ac.in.
