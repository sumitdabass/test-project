## Overview of Changes for B.PT and B.OT

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has issued a formal notification regarding modifications to the Bachelor of Physiotherapy (B.PT) and Bachelor of Occupational Therapy (B.OT) programs. These updates are applicable for the upcoming 2026-27 academic session.

## Key Updates in Nomenclature and Criteria

The university has revised the nomenclature for these paramedical courses. Additionally, the admission criteria, which dictate eligibility and selection processes, have been updated. Prospective students should review these changes carefully before applying.

For a broader understanding of the university's entry requirements, students can refer to the [IPU admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php). It is also recommended to keep an eye on the [IPU CET cutoff](/ipu-cet-cutoff-2025.php) trends for related health science programs.

## Official Notification

Students are advised to check the detailed PDF on the official university website (ipu.ac.in or ipu.admissions.nic.in) to verify specific eligibility requirements and the updated course titles. Refer to the official notification dated 21.04.2026 for the complete list of changes.
