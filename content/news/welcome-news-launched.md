## Welcome

This is the new dedicated section for IPU admission news and announcements.

## What you'll see here

- **Counselling rounds** — schedules, seat allotment dates, document requirements
- **CET updates** — exam dates, admit cards, results
- **Admission notifications** — new programmes, eligibility changes, deadline extensions
- **Results** — merit lists, cutoffs, final rankings

## Where the updates come from

Content here is sourced from official IPU channels, not third-party editorial:

- [ipu.ac.in](https://ipu.ac.in) — the university's main notification page
- [ipuadmissions.nic.in](https://ipuadmissions.nic.in) — the admission portal
- Google News feeds filtered to "IPU admission" and "GGSIPU counselling"

Real updates start appearing here shortly.
