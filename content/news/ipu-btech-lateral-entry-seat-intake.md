## Seat Intake for B.Tech Lateral Entry Programs

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has published the official seat matrix for Lateral Entry to B.Tech programs for the 2026-27 academic session. This notification applies to candidates applying under CET Code 128 (Diploma Holders) and CET Code 129 (B.Sc. Graduates).

## Breakdown of Seats

The seat intake includes allocations for various affiliated colleges and university schools. It specifically details the seats available under the Management Quota (MQ). Prospective students should review these figures to understand the availability of seats in their respective branches before the [GGSIPU counselling for B-Tech admission](/GGSIPU-counselling-for-B-Tech-admission.php) begins.

### CET Codes Covered

- **CET Code 128:** Lateral Entry to B.Tech for Diploma holders.
- **CET Code 129:** Lateral Entry to B.Tech for B.Sc. graduates.

Candidates are encouraged to refer to the official university portal at ipu.ac.in for the specific college-wise distribution. For more general information on the process, you can refer to the [IPU admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php) or the specific [IPU B.Tech admission 2025](/IPU-B-Tech-admission-2025.php) overview.
