## Official Seat Intake for B.Tech Lateral Entry

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has released the detailed seat allocation and intake capacity for the B.Tech Lateral Entry programs for the 2026-27 academic session. This announcement is critical for candidates who have applied under the Lateral Entry scheme to join engineering programs in the second year.

### Coverage of CET Codes

The seat matrix applies to the following Common Entrance Test (CET) categories:
- **CET Code 128:** Lateral Entry to B.Tech for Diploma Holders.
- **CET Code 129:** Lateral Entry to B.Tech for B.Sc. Graduates.

Candidates can use this information to understand the availability of seats across various affiliated institutes and university schools before participating in the [GGSIPU counselling for B-Tech admission](/GGSIPU-counselling-for-B-Tech-admission.php).

## Management Quota and Allocation

The released data includes the intake for the Management Quota (MQ) alongside regular seats. Prospective students should review the [IPU admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php) to understand the eligibility criteria and reservation policies applicable to these seats.

## Next Steps for Candidates

1. **Verify Intake:** Check the official PDF for the specific number of seats available in your preferred engineering branch.
2. **Prepare for Counselling:** Ensure all documentation is ready for the upcoming verification rounds.
3. **Monitor Updates:** Stay informed about the schedule for choice filling and seat allotment by visiting the official university website regularly.
