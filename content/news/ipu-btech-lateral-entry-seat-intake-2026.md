## B.Tech Lateral Entry Seat Intake Details

GGSIPU has released the seat allocation and intake for B.Tech Lateral Entry programs for the 2026 academic session. This notification applies to candidates seeking direct entry into the second year of engineering through two specific pathways: Diploma holders and B.Sc graduates.

### Intake for CET Code 128 and 129

The official document provides a comprehensive breakdown of available seats across affiliated institutes. This intake encompasses various engineering branches. Candidates should review these details carefully before participating in the [GGSIPU counselling for B-Tech admission](/GGSIPU-counselling-for-B-Tech-admission.php).

*   **CET Code 128:** For candidates holding a Diploma in Engineering.
*   **CET Code 129:** For candidates who are B.Sc graduates.

### Management Quota (MQ) Seats

The seat matrix specifically includes the intake for the Management Quota (MQ). Students seeking admission under this category should consult the [IPU admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php) for general procedural details, while specific MQ applications are typically handled at the institutional level as per university norms.

### Important Considerations

Candidates are advised to verify the seat distribution for their specific category and branch before participating in the choice-filling process. For reference on previous admission trends and requirements, students may check the [IPU CET cutoff](/ipu-cet-cutoff-2025.php) details. All candidates must refer to the official university website at ipu.ac.in for the complete list of participating colleges and their respective seat counts.
