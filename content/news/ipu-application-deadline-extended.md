## Extension of Online Application Dates

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has officially announced an extension for the submission of online application forms for the 2026-27 academic session. This update applies to candidates seeking admission through National Level Tests and those applying for merit-based programs.

Prospective students who missed the previous window now have an additional opportunity to register. For a comprehensive overview of the process, you can refer to our [IPU admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php).

### Impacted Admission Categories

The extension covers two primary pathways for entry into the university:

1. **National Level Test Based Admissions:** This includes various undergraduate and postgraduate courses where scores from national exams are mandatory. For instance, those interested in management can check the requirements for [MBA admission at IP University](/mba-admission-ip-university.php).
2. **Merit-Based Admissions:** This applies to specific programs where selection is determined by the marks obtained in the qualifying examination rather than a dedicated entrance test.

### Important Instructions for Applicants

Candidates are advised to complete their registration on the official portal (ipu.admissions.nic.in) as soon as possible. Ensure all documents are uploaded correctly and the application fee is processed before the revised deadline. 

Following the application phase, students should prepare for the next steps in the enrollment cycle, including [GGSIPU counselling](/GGSIPU-counselling-for-B-Tech-admission.php) for relevant technical and professional courses.
