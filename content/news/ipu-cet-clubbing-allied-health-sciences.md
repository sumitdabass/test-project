## Combined CET for Paramedical Courses

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has notified a change in the entrance examination structure for several allied health science programs. The university has decided to club the Common Entrance Tests (CET) for programs originally categorized under Code 119 and Code 124.

## List of Clubbed Programs

The following undergraduate programs will now be covered under a single combined entrance test:

- **B.PT** (Bachelor of Physiotherapy)
- **B.OT** (Bachelor of Occupational Therapy)
- **BASLP** (Bachelor of Audiology and Speech-Language Pathology)
- **BPO** (Bachelor of Prosthetics and Orthotics)
- **B.MLS** (Bachelor in Medical Laboratory Science)

## Important Information for Candidates

This administrative change means that students interested in these specific paramedical fields will sit for one unified exam. For more information on how to prepare for university entrance exams, visit the [IPU admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php). Additionally, candidates can check previous [IPU CET cutoff](/ipu-cet-cutoff-2025.php) data to gauge the required scores for these programs.

For specific details regarding the exam date and syllabus for the combined test, candidates are advised to refer to the official notification at ipu.ac.in.
