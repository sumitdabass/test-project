## Deadline Extension for IPU Admissions 2026

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has announced an extension for the submission of online application forms for the 2026-27 academic session. This extension applies to candidates seeking admission through National Level Tests and merit-based pathways.

## Steps for Applicants

Prospective students who missed the previous deadline now have additional time to complete their registration. It is recommended to check the [IPU admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php) for general requirements before applying. For those interested in specific professional courses like management, you can review the [MBA admission details](/mba-admission-ip-university.php) to understand the national-level entrance requirements.

## Verification of Dates

While the extension has been confirmed, candidates are advised to check the exact closing date and time on the official university portal. For the most accurate and updated schedule, refer to the official notification at ipu.ac.in.
