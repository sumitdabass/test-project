## Final Call for CET 2026 Registration

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has released a notification regarding the final window for CET registration. This serves as the absolute last opportunity for prospective students to apply for various undergraduate and postgraduate programs that require the university's entrance examination.

## Important Information for Applicants

Candidates who missed the earlier deadlines are advised to complete their registration immediately to avoid missing the current academic cycle. This extension is applicable to all courses where admission is conducted via the IPU CET. 

For a comprehensive overview of the process, you can refer to our [IPU admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php). 

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official GGSIPU admission website.
2. Fill in the required personal and academic details in the registration form.
3. Upload the necessary documents as per the specified format.
4. Pay the application fee and download the confirmation page.

Students interested in specific technical programs should also check the latest updates on [IPU B.Tech admission 2026](/IPU-B-Tech-admission-2025.php) or the requirements for [MCA admission IPU](/mca-admission-ipu.php). For further details and the official schedule, candidates are encouraged to refer to the official notification at ipu.ac.in.
