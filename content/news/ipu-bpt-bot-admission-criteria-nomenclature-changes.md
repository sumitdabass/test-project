## New Guidelines for BPT and BOT Admissions

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has issued a formal notification regarding the Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) and Bachelor of Occupational Therapy (BOT) programs. For the 2026-27 academic session, the university has introduced changes to the nomenclature and specific admission criteria for these courses.

## Key Changes for Prospective Students

Candidates planning to apply for these paramedical programs must review the updated requirements to ensure they meet the revised eligibility standards. These modifications are part of the university's updated framework for the upcoming academic year.

### Admission and Eligibility

The revised criteria will govern the selection process for the 2026-27 intake. Students are encouraged to consult the [ipu admission guide](/ipu-admission-guide.php) for a general overview of the university's application procedures. 

For the specific technical details regarding the nomenclature changes and the exact eligibility benchmarks, candidates should refer to the official notification dated April 21, 2026, available on the university's admission portal. It is recommended to verify all details at ipu.ac.in before proceeding with the application.
